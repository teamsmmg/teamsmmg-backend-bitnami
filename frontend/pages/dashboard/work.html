Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of projects do you work on? +

We focus on AI solutions, web and app development, and impactful innovations during hackathons. Our projects range from cutting-edge machine learning applications to responsive web platforms.

Can I collaborate with your team? +

We're always open to new collaborations. Contact us through the "Collaborate With Us" section above, and we'll get back to you to discuss potential partnerships and projects.

What tools and technologies do you use? +

Our stack includes React, Node.js, Python, and cutting-edge AI tools. We're always exploring new technologies to stay at the forefront of innovation in software development and artificial intelligence.

Do you participate in virtual hackathons? +

Yes, we actively participate in both virtual and in-person events. Virtual hackathons allow us to collaborate with a diverse group of developers and innovators from around the world.
